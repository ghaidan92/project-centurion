OUR STORY

·

Established in San Francisco and founded by Raul Vinces, CFS began in 2006 as Centurion Janitorial Services. Throughout the years, the CFS team has served clients all over the San Francisco Bay Area and have taken on projects big and small to provide the utmost service to all of our clients. Our multi-disciplinary approach has transformed CFS into a leading performer in the property maintenance and management industry. Centurion Facility Services has clients ranging from commercial offices, homeowners associations, clinics, retail spaces, fitness centers, churches, salon and spas, and more, as part of our great continuing service.
